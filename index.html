STEP 3

Core Modules — Built Around Workflows

NOT random features. Focused workflows.

1. AI Business Assistant

The intelligence layer. Answers business questions, guides users, provides strategic assistance, simplifies workflows.

2. Business Planning Studio

Generate business plans, structure ideas, define models, create strategies. From idea to structured plan in minutes.

3. Proposal & Document Generator

Generate proposals, reports, concept notes, presentations, investor summaries. Huge value for SMEs, consultants, NGOs, startups.

4. Investor Readiness Toolkit

Prepare pitch decks, improve financial positioning, structure investment materials. Get funding-ready.

5. Productivity Workspace

Simple: tasks, notes, execution support, workflow organization. NOT overcomplicated.

STEP 4

Strategic Advantage

NOT "better AI"

Our advantage is: African business workflow intelligence.

Localized realities, startup struggles, operational simplicity, accessibility, business growth context. That is our moat.

STEP 5

STEP 6

MVP Philosophy

Business Planning + Execution + Investor Readiness

That alone is already a powerful product.
