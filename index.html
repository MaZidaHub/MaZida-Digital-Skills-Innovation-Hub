About MaZida

Digital skills, AI infrastructure & growth ecosystem for Africa

MaZida Digital Skills & Innovation Hub is an African innovation ecosystem platform focused on empowering businesses, startups, and professionals through AI-powered systems, digital skills, and growth infrastructure. Operated by Basiwel Holistic Ventures Ltd, we develop MaZida AI Workspace and Startup Media initiatives. We champion financial inclusion by helping underserved African SMEs and startups access AI-powered business planning, operational execution & support, and investor-readiness tools — traditionally inaccessible or expensive.


MaZida AI builds AI-powered productivity tools for African SMEs: business planning, proposal generation, operational execution. GPU compute ensures scalable document analysis and future AI model optimization.
