Product Vision

To provide African businesses and professionals with an AI-powered workspace that simplifies planning, execution, operations, and growth through intelligent productivity systems.

PHASE 2

Core Users

1. SMEs

Need: structure, planning, operations support.

2. Startups (High-growth)

Need: investor readiness, business models, execution systems, pitch preparation.

3. Consultants & Freelancers

Need: proposals, reports, productivity, client workflows.

4. Young Professionals

Need: productivity, AI assistance, growth support, digital tools.

PHASE 3

Core Modules — Built Around Workflows

Do NOT build random features. Build around workflows.

1. AI Business Assistant

The intelligence layer. Functions: answer business questions, guide users, provide strategic assistance, simplify workflows.

2. Business Planning Studio

Users can: generate business plans, structure ideas, define models, create strategies.

3. Proposal & Document Generator

Generate: proposals, reports, concept notes, presentations, investor summaries. Huge value for: SMEs, consultants, NGOs, startups.

4. Investor Readiness Toolkit

Help startups: prepare pitch decks, improve financial positioning, structure investment materials.

5. Productivity Workspace

Simple: tasks, notes, execution support, workflow organization. Do NOT overcomplicate initially.

African Business Workflow Intelligence

Meaning: localized realities, startup struggles, operational simplicity, accessibility, business growth context.

That is our moat.
